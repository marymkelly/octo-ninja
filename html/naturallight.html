Natural Light

Natural Light also known as Natty, was introduced in 1977. It was the first light beer to have reduced calories. It became a quick selling, top selling beer in America due to its higher ABV. Natty a big hit in America when compared to Bud Light. It won the bronze medal in 2008 for the "American-style light lager".

Natural Light has an ABV of 4.2%, 95 grams of calories, 3.2 grams of carbs, and 0.7 grams of protein.

Fun Fact


Natural Light was the first beer to go into space. Check it out!
