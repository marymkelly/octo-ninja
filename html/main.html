Who we are

When it comes to alcohol, we want our campus to be safe and understand the types of alcohol that you can come into contact with. The three types of alcohol include beer, liquor, and liqueur. We will describe how these types are made and the differences between them. This will also bring about examples such as Bud Light, Vodka, and Malibu.
