Bud Light

Budweiser Light was actually brought in in 1982. This particular beer was shown from 1994 to 1997 in 30 second commericials that had featured Rob and Laura from The Dick Van Dyke Show.

With Bud Light, most of America purchases this particular beer for parties and football games. The grains consist of barley malt and cereals, the alcohol percentage is at 4.2% by the volume.

It has 110 calories with 0 grams of fat and 6.6 grams of carbs per fluid ounce. It also has 0.9 grams of protein.

Since the brewing is a natural process, the values shown above are approximate and can be slightly different.

Fun Fact

Bud Light maintains the same percentage of ABV and the same calories at 110 from when it was introduced.
